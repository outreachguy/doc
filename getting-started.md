# Getting Started

Get up and running with OutreachGuy in just a few minutes.

## Create your account

1. Go to the OutreachGuy website and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address

That's it—you're in!

## Complete onboarding

When you first log in, we'll walk you through a quick setup:

### Step 1: Choose your account type
Are you a **Company/Brand** or a **Creator/Influencer**? This helps us tailor the experience for you.

### Step 2: Select your goals
Pick up to 4 goals you want to achieve:
- Post consistently
- Reply to relevant posts
- Grow your followers
- Find leads
- And more...

### Step 3: Connect your X account
Link your X (Twitter) account so OutreachGuy can post and engage on your behalf. Don't worry—you control everything.

### Step 4: Review your powers
We'll show you the quick actions available. You can customize these later.

### Step 5: Activate your missions
Based on your goals, we'll suggest some missions to get you started. Toggle on the ones you want to run.

### Step 6: Choose a plan
Pick the plan that fits your needs. You can start with a free trial to test things out.

## Your dashboard

After onboarding, you'll land on your **Home** dashboard. Here you'll see:

- **Your impact** — How your outreach is performing
- **Active missions** — What's currently running
- **Quick actions** — Powers you can run right now
- **Recent activity** — What's been happening

## What's next?

Now that you're set up:
- Learn about [Missions](missions.md) to automate your outreach
- Explore [Powers](powers.md) for quick actions
- Check your [Activity](activity.md) to see how things are going

You're ready to grow!
