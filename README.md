# Welcome to OutreachGuy

OutreachGuy is your AI-powered assistant for growing on X (Twitter). It helps you find potential customers, engage with your audience, and build your presence—all on autopilot.

## Who is OutreachGuy for?

- **Companies and brands** looking to generate leads and grow their social presence
- **Creators and influencers** who want to engage more with their audience
- **Marketers** who need to scale their outreach without spending hours online

## What can you do with OutreachGuy?

- **Automate your outreach** — Set up missions that post, reply, and engage for you
- **Find leads** — Discover people who are interested in what you offer
- **Save time** — Let AI handle the repetitive work while you focus on your business
- **Track your growth** — See your engagement and follower growth in one place
