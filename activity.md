# Activity & Analytics

Track what OutreachGuy is doing for you and see how your account is growing.

## Your dashboard

The Home page shows your key metrics at a glance:

- **Impact stats** — Total posts, replies, DMs, and engagement
- **Activity charts** — Visual trends of your growth over time
- **Active missions** — What's currently running

## Activity timeline

See everything that's happened:

1. Go to **Activity** in the sidebar
2. Browse the timeline of all actions

Each activity shows:
- What action was taken (post, reply, DM)
- When it happened
- The content that was posted
- Engagement it received

### Filtering activities

Use the filters to find specific activities:
- **Type** — Posts, replies, DMs, or lead captures
- **Date** — Show activities from a specific period
- **Search** — Find activities containing specific text

## Understanding your stats

### Posts
How many tweets you've posted through OutreachGuy.

### Replies
Replies sent to other people's tweets.

### DMs
Direct messages sent to leads and contacts.

### Views
How many people saw your content.

### Likes
Likes received on your posts and replies.

### Followers
Your total follower count and how it's changing.

## Tracking growth

The charts on your dashboard show trends over time:
- **Follower growth** — Are you gaining followers consistently?
- **Engagement rate** — How many people interact with your content?
- **Activity volume** — How much outreach is being done?

Check these regularly to see what's working.

## Refreshing metrics

Engagement numbers update automatically, but you can refresh them manually:
1. Find the post in your Activity
2. Click the refresh icon
3. Latest engagement numbers will load

## Tips

- Check your activity at least once a week
- Look for patterns—what type of content gets the most engagement?
- If growth slows down, try adjusting your mission briefs
- Use the stats to report your social media ROI
