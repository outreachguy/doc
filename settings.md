# Settings

Customize OutreachGuy to work the way you want.

## Accessing settings

Click the **gear icon** in the sidebar, then select **Settings**.

## Profile

Manage your account information:

- **Name** — Your display name
- **Email** — Your login email
- **Avatar** — Your profile picture

## Plan

View and manage your subscription:

- **Current plan** — What you're subscribed to
- **Energy balance** — How much energy you have left
- **Usage** — How much you've used this month
- **Auto-recharge** — Toggle automatic energy refills
- **Upgrade/Downgrade** — Change your plan

See [Plans & Billing](billing.md) for more details.

## Notifications

Control what notifications you receive:

- **Email notifications** — Updates sent to your email
- **In-app notifications** — Alerts inside OutreachGuy

Choose what you want to be notified about:
- Mission completions
- New leads found
- Account activity
- System updates

## Data & Knowledge

Manage the information OutreachGuy knows about your project:

- **Restart onboarding** — Go through setup again
- **Rescan website** — Update business info from your website
- **Project identity** — Edit your brand details
- **Custom fields** — Add custom data fields for leads

## Integrations

Connect OutreachGuy with other tools:

View and manage any third-party integrations you've set up.

## Billing

Access your payment information:

- **Payment method** — Update your card
- **Invoices** — View and download past invoices
- **Billing history** — See all charges

## Logging out

To log out:
1. Click your profile in the sidebar
2. Select **Log out**

You'll be returned to the login page.

## Deleting your account

If you need to delete your account, contact support through the in-app chat. We'll help you with the process and ensure your data is removed.
